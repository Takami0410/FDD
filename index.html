Welcome to Our Futsal Community

Our community is a place where students from different countries and cultures come together to enjoy football and futsal.
We believe that sports can connect people and create friendships beyond nationality and background.

Our Activities

Play Together

We enjoy football and futsal together regardless of nationality or culture. Our community is open to everyone who loves the game.

Sessions

During our sessions, around 20 players join and we divide them into teams to play friendly matches.

Competitions

We regularly organize competitions and also participate in tournaments with other teams.
